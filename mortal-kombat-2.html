About the Project

The sequel to the smash-hit 2021 reboot, Mortal Kombat II sees Earth's greatest champions face off against the forces of Outworld in a high-stakes battle for survival. Produced for Warner Bros., the film raises the bar for the franchise with larger-scale action and an expanded roster of iconic fighters.

Filmed in Queensland, Australia, the production draws on the country's world-class facilities and significant government incentives.
