/ The Building

We Own
the Building.

750 square metres of purpose-built VFX production space in Marrickville, Sydney — a former smash repairs workshop, rebuilt from the floor up for the work we do. Six-metre ceilings. Three-phase power. Solar generation with on-site Tesla Powerwall battery backup — we run on our own power. The grid going down is not our problem.

Not renting. Not co-working. Not one lease renewal away from disrupting your project. This is a permanent facility, owned by the people running it — built to handle the heaviest production demands without blinking.

/ Certification & Incentives
TPN Gold Certified.
Australia's Only Independent.

The Trusted Partner Network Gold certification is the entertainment industry's highest standard for content security — required on the biggest productions in the world. Your IP, your assets, your pipeline: protected to Hollywood specifications.

Future Associate is the only independent VFX studio in Australia to hold this certification. Every other TPN Gold facility in this country sits inside a larger corporate structure. We don't. For productions with strict security and delivery requirements, there is no independent alternative.

/ Leadership
Lindsay
Adams
Founder & Head of Studio

Lindsay Adams founded Future Associate in 2018 with a clear mandate: deliver Hollywood-grade VFX at exceptional quality, without the bloat of traditional studio structures. The result is a lean, TPN Gold-certified operation that punches well above its weight on the world stage.

With over two decades in the industry, Lindsay has worked at the highest level — cutting his teeth at Animal Logic, Weta Digital, ILM, MPC, Method Studios and Rising Sun Pictures — contributing to some of the most technically demanding productions ever made. He was a key member of the Oscar-nominated VFX team on Mad Max: Fury Road and received a VES Award nomination for the same film, alongside an APDG Award nomination for Best Visual Effects Design for The Leftovers: Season 3.

As Production VFX Supervisor, Lindsay has worked shoulder-to-shoulder with directors and producers on set — from Crazy Rich Asians to HBO's Watchmen — translating creative vision into final pixels with precision and speed.
