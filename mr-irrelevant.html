StudioParamount Pictures
Year2026

About the Project

The true story of John Tuggle — the last pick of the 1983 NFL Draft, branded forever as "Mr. Irrelevant." Directed by Jonathan Levine and starring David Corenswet as Tuggle alongside Michael Shannon as Bill Parcells, the film follows a man written off by the league who carved out a place in the hearts of the New York Giants.

A Skydance Sports production, set for Christmas Day release through Paramount Pictures.
