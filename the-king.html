About the Project

David Michôd's sweeping adaptation of Shakespeare's Henriad — a gripping tale of power, war and betrayal set in medieval England. Hal, a wayward prince who has rejected the crown, is thrust onto the throne following his father's death. Starring Timothée Chalamet, Joel Edgerton, and Robert Pattinson.

A Netflix original film, The King is a stately, immersive historical epic — built around an extraordinary central performance and the brutal logic of medieval warfare.
